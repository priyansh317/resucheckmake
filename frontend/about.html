About RESUCHECKMAKE

RESUCHECKMAKE is an AI-powered resume analysis and resume creation platform built to help job seekers stand out in today’s competitive hiring process.

Most companies use Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) to automatically screen resumes before a recruiter ever sees them. Unfortunately, many qualified candidates get rejected simply because their resumes are not ATS-optimized.

Our platform solves this problem by analyzing your resume against job descriptions, calculating an ATS match score, identifying missing keywords, and helping you create clean, professional, and ATS-friendly resumes in minutes.

Our mission is simple: to make resume building, job matching, and career growth easy, accurate, and accessible for everyone — students, freshers, and experienced professionals alike.

With RESUCHECKMAKE, you don’t need design skills, paid tools, or complex software — just your details and your career goals.
